Welcome to Chenlin Gu's homepage.

I am an assistant professor at YMSC of Tsinghua University since September 2022.

My research interest is probability theory, and especially focuses on homogenization and its interactions with various stochastic models including random walk in random environment, percolation, interacting particle systems, etc.

From 2021 to 2022, I am a postdoctoral instructor at NYU Shanghai, mentored by Wei Wu. From 2018 to 2021, I did my PhD at DMA/ENS, supervised by Jean-Christophe Mourrat. Before starting PhD, I finished my master at Uinversité Paris-Sud, engineer diplome at Ecole Polytechnique and bachelor at Fudan University.
